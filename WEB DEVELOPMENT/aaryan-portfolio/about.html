About Aaryan

Aaryan Mali is a UX designer with five years of experience creating intuitive products across web and mobile platforms. Their background combines user research, interaction design, and collaborative problem-solving with cross-functional teams. Aaryan's design philosophy centers on clarity, empathy, and continuous iteration, and they love UX work because it turns real user pain points into smooth, meaningful digital experiences.
